General Information

As part of the University's continued effort to serve its current and former students in a more efficient way, Bowen University is introducing a new way for you to place your transcript orders using the Bowen Online Transcript Application (BOTA).

Our Bowen Online Transcript Application is available now for Bowen University.
 This service is applicable for Returning Students of the University 
 This service is also applicable for Existing Students of the University. 
 Postgraduate Alumni should please send their transcript application to the School of Post Graduate Studies. 
 Alumni from affiliate schools should please send their transcript application to their respective affiliate institution for processing.

Using this platform, there is no longer a need for you to visit the school premises or send a friend or relative to apply for your academic transcripts; saving time and money.

Bowen Online Transcript Application (BOTA) deliver transcripts both electronic and paper versions, depending on what time and order is placed. An electronic could be delivered in a secure online process the same day or next business day. A paper transcript could also be processed the same day or the next business day and will be delivered through courier services at additional cost.

Current Students and Alumni are able to:

 Order a transcript 24/7 from anywhere in the world with internet access.
 Request an official electronically-accessed transcript or mailed paper transcript.
 Confirm an electronic signature and payment through a secure online portal.
 Receive email notifications if a hold is preventing the release of a transcript.
 Receive email notifications when orders are processed and electronic transcripts are downloaded.
