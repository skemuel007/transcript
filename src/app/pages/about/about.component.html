About Bowen Online Transcript Application (BOTA)

With regards to the university's continued efforts to serve its students (present and former) in a more effective way, Bowen University has introduced a new way for you to make your transcript request using the Bowen Online Transcript Application (BOTA) platform. The platform will provide the possibility to make request and pay for your academic transcript online, and where applicable, such transcripts will be delivered electronically.

Using this platform, there is no longer a need for you to visit the school premises or send a friend or relative to apply for your academic transcripts; saving time and money.

Bowen Online Transcript Application (BOTA) deliver transcripts both electronic and paper versions, depending on what time and order is placed. An electronic could be delivered in a secure online process the same day or next business day. A paper transcript could also be processed the same day or the next business day and will be delivered through courier services at additional cost.

To begin, you will need to register by clicking on the " Apply for Transcript" link at the top of the webpage or click on the login button, if you have already registered.
