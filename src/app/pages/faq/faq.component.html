Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Top 5 Questions:
How do I order Bowen University transcript?  

You can request for your transcript through the Bowen Online Transcript Application (BOTA). You will need to request one transcript for each university / organisation that you want to apply to. For more information, see the Application Procedures section of the Bowen Transcript page. 

How do I pay for my application?  

You can pay for your application online with VISA, MasterCard and VERVE Card.
For our mailing address, see our Contact Us page.

How do I get a copy of my transcript if I've graduated?  

If you are a graduate of Bowen University, using the Bowen Online Transcript Application (BOTA), you will be able to request a transcript as a step in your online application through Apply for Transcript. For more information on requesting your transcripts, see the Application Procedures section of the Bowen Transcript page.

What do I do if I am having problem with my application?  

You can get help with the Bowen Online Transcript Application in the following ways:
Check the FAQ section of this FAQ for your question.
Check the Application Procedures section for the step-by-step instructions.
Contact the Help Desk for assistance via telephone or message using the Contact Us section.

How will I find out when my transcript has been sent?  

You are expected to provide shipping details about the transcript you are requesting. You will then receive a mail stating that your transcript is been processed or has been shipped.  For more enquires visit our Contact Us page.
